HOW IT WORKS

APPLICATION SYSTEMS & PROJECT APPROACH

Shoreline Exterior Systems is a full-service residential and commercial exterior contractor. We are experienced applicators of industry-leading EIFS, stucco, and exterior finish systems used across a wide range of project types, including commercial buildings, multi-family developments, healthcare facilities, educational institutions, and custom homes.

01

Pre-Construction Services

Our most successful projects begin during preliminary design. We collaborate closely with owners, architects, and general contractors on detailing, budgeting, system selection, and constructability analysis to ensure long-term performance.

02

Education & Collaboration

We offer Lunch-and-Learns and formal presentations for design teams and construction professionals, covering the latest exterior system technologies, best practices, and installation standards.

Inspections & Long-Term Protection

To protect asset value, we provide annual inspections, maintenance guidance, and long-term warranty support for exterior systems we install.
