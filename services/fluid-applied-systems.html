Continuous Building Envelope Protection

Fluid-applied air and moisture barrier systems create a seamless, fully adhered layer of protection behind exterior finishes. These systems control air leakage and prevent moisture intrusion at the wall assembly level.

Proper installation is critical to long-term performance and system compatibility with stucco, EIFS, and other cladding systems.

Manufacturer-Approved Installation

Our team installs fluid-applied systems in accordance with manufacturer specifications, ensuring proper thickness, curing, detailing, and transitions.

These systems are commonly used in new construction and remediation projects to enhance durability and performance.
