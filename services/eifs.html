High-Performance EIFS Solutions

Exterior Insulation and Finish Systems (EIFS) provide superior insulation, moisture control, and architectural flexibility. When properly installed, EIFS delivers excellent thermal performance and long-term durability.

Shoreline Exterior Systems installs manufacturer-approved EIFS assemblies with proper drainage, flashing integration, and detailing.

Installation & Remediation

Our EIFS services include new installations, repairs, moisture remediation, and system upgrades. We focus on proper substrate preparation, drainage planes, and termination detailing.

EIFS is commonly used in commercial buildings, multi-family developments, and modern residential construction.
