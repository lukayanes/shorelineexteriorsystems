Exterior Contracting Services in Sarasota, FL

Shoreline Exterior Systems provides comprehensive exterior contracting services including traditional cement stucco, Exterior Insulation and Finish Systems (EIFS), manufactured stone veneer, thin brick applications, exterior repairs and restoration, fluid-applied air and moisture barrier systems, flashing and sealant installation, stucco inspections, and custom architectural foam shapes and trim. Our services are designed to enhance durability, appearance, and long-term performance for both residential and commercial properties.

Traditional Stucco Services

Professional installation and repair of traditional cement stucco systems built for durability, weather resistance, and long-term exterior performance.

Exterior Insulation Finish System (EIFS) Services

High-performance EIFS solutions designed to improve insulation, moisture control, and modern exterior appearance for residential and commercial buildings.

Manufactured Stone Veneer Services

Architectural stone veneer installations that enhance curb appeal, add texture, and create a premium exterior finish.

Repairs and Restoration

Exterior repair and restoration services addressing cracks, surface damage, water intrusion, and aging stucco or finish systems.

Fluid-Applied Air and Moisture Systems

Seamless air and moisture barrier systems applied to protect building envelopes and prevent long-term water intrusion.

Flashing and Sealants

Precision flashing and sealant installation to properly terminate exterior systems and safeguard structural integrity.

Stucco Inspection and Treatment

Comprehensive stucco inspections to identify moisture issues, system failures, and necessary repairs before major damage occurs.

Architectural Foam Shapes and Trims

Custom architectural foam shapes and trim details that enhance exterior design and elevate the finished appearance of your property.
